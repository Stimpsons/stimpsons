About Us

StimpSons provides objective, data-driven analysis of golf course greens. By combining stimpmeter measurements with professional assessments, we deliver accurate ratings that help courses maintain consistency, fairness, and championship-level performance.

What We Measure

Stimp Speed

Precise stimpmeter readings to evaluate green speed and consistency.

Smoothness & Roll

Assessment of surface quality, ball roll, and imperfections.

Slope & Fairness

Evaluation of slopes, pin placements, and playability.

Overall Rating

A clear, easy-to-understand green rating for players and course managers.
